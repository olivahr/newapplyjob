Work Schedules & Shift Structure

Warehouse operations run on fixed shifts to support production. You may be assigned to one of three shifts. Shifts may be scheduled as 8-hour or 10-hour workdays depending on business needs. Start times and days worked depend on the role and department.

First Shift (Day Shift)

Day Shift supports daytime production and warehouse operations. This shift typically starts in the morning. Schedules may be 8 or 10 hours depending on the department.

Second Shift (Evening Shift)

Evening Shift supports afternoon/evening production and helps keep operations moving into the night. Start times are later than Day Shift. Schedules may be 8 or 10 hours depending on needs.

Third Shift (Overnight Shift)

Overnight Shift supports continuous production through the night. This shift runs late evening into early morning. Schedules may be 8 or 10 hours depending on production requirements.

Overtime and weekend scheduling may be required during peak production periods. All scheduling is based on operational demand.

*Important Scheduling Notice: Shift selection happens after you are approved through the hiring process. If approved, you will receive access to the employee portal where available shifts can be reviewed. Shift availability depends on business needs and what is open at the time of onboarding.
